Free Wi-Fi

The cafe provides free Wi-Fi access, allowing customers to work or browse the internet while enjoying their coffee. We wish to provide a peaceful place for you to work.

The cafe engages with the local community, supporting literacy programs and donating books to schools and libraries to futher promote literacy.

Events and workshops

The cafe hosts various events and workshops, including book clubs, and writing workshops, providing a platform for literary enthusiasts to connect and engage.
